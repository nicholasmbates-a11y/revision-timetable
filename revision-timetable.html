Add your Subjects
First, type in each subject you're studying — for example Maths, Physics, Chemistry. Pick a unique colour for each one so it's easy to identify at a glance.

You can add up to 12 subjects — enough for a full GCSE timetable. You can also edit your subjects later at any time from the main view.

Step 2 — 3 of 9
Add your Modules
For each subject, add all the topics or modules you need to revise. You can type them one at a time, or use Paste multiple modules at once to drop in a whole list in seconds — one module per line.

Don't worry about getting every module perfect right now — you can always edit them later.

Step 3 — 5 of 9
Plan your Daily Sessions
Click a week to expand it into large day tiles. Each day has three session slots: Morning ☀, Lunch 🌿, and Afternoon 🌙.

For each slot, pick a subject from the dropdown — then choose the specific module you'll be revising. Collapsed weeks show colour-coded dots so you can see your whole month at a glance.

Gamification — 6 of 9
Level up your Mastery
The app tracks two things as you revise:

⭐ Points — earn 100 points every time you complete a week by covering 3 different modules from each subject.

Mastery Level — your level in each subject goes up when every single module has been revised that many times. Level 1 means you've hit every module once, Level 2 means twice, and so on.

Smart Features — 7 of 9
Stay on track with Smart Tools
✓ Mark Done — tick off each session as you actually complete it. Only ticked sessions count toward points and mastery, so the numbers reflect real revision.

✨ Suggest button — let the app pick your most-neglected module to fill in any session slot.

🔥 Day Streak — track consecutive days of revision. 🏆 Achievements — unlock 12 badges for hitting milestones (streaks, levels, weeks, sessions). ⚠ Neglected alerts appear on subjects with modules untouched for 2+ weeks.

📅 Exam dates — set an exam date per subject and watch a countdown appear.

Settings & Tools — 8 of 9
Make it Yours
The top bar always has these settings ready:

🌙 / ☀️ Dark or Light mode — switch any time to suit your eyes or environment.

⊞ Patterns — toggles colour-blind friendly hatching patterns onto each subject, so they're identifiable without relying on colour alone.

🖨 Print — generates a clean, paper-friendly version of your full month with every week expanded. Use it to print to paper or "Save as PDF" to keep an offline copy or stick it on your wall.

❓ Help — opens this tutorial again at any time. ⬇ Export / ⬆ Import in the main view back up and restore your data. 📍 Jump to Today snaps you back to the current week instantly.

Pro Features — 9 of 9
Take it Further
✨ Smart Suggest — uses spaced repetition. The longer ago you revised something (and the lower your confidence), the higher its priority.

+ Add another module — fit two modules into one session for longer revision blocks.

★ Confidence rating — after marking a session done, rate how well you understood it (1-5 stars). Low-confidence modules get suggested more often.

📝 Notes — jot down what you covered or where you got stuck. Builds a study journal.

🌴 Rest Days — tap any date or set recurring weekly rest days in ⚙ Settings. They don't break your streak.

📅 Calendar export — download your sessions as a .ics file to import into Google or Apple Calendar with reminders.

⚙ Settings — adjust your weekly target (auto-scales to 60% of your subjects, or fix it) and manage rest days.
